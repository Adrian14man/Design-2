Our story

Built on relationships and quality work.

AD Construction LLC was founded with a simple goal — give Colorado Springs homeowners a contractor they can actually trust. After more than ten years working in the trades, we know that the difference between an okay job and a great one comes down to prep, attention to detail, and showing up when you say you will.

We're family-operated and hands-on. When you call, you talk to someone who'll actually be on your job. We've handled everything from single-room remodels to large-scale work like 300-home repaints on military bases — and every project gets the same care, whether it's one bathroom or forty units.

Most of our work comes from referrals — neighbors who liked the result and told a friend. That's the kind of business we want to keep building.
