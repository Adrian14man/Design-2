Our services

What we build, repair, and finish

Full-scope construction services for Colorado Springs homeowners — handled by a small, experienced team.

01

Interior & Exterior Painting

Clean, long-lasting paintwork starts with proper preparation. We patch, sand, prime, and protect surfaces before any color goes on, then apply two full coats with attention to cut lines and finish quality. We handle everything from a single accent wall to full home repaints, inside and out.

  • Whole-home interior repaints
  • Exterior siding and trim
  • Cabinet refinishing
  • Drywall texture and patch repairs
  • Color consultation
  • Pressure washing and prep
02

Drywall Installation & Finishing

Smooth walls and clean corners take experience. We hang, tape, mud, and sand drywall to a level finish that's ready for paint or texture, and we repair damage of any size — water, holes, settling cracks, or full sections. Whether it's one bedroom or a full basement, the finish is consistent.

  • New drywall installation
  • Tape, mud, and sanding
  • Texture matching (knockdown, orange peel, smooth)
  • Hole and water damage repair
  • Ceiling repairs
  • Basement and remodel finishing
03

Remodeling

From kitchen overhauls to bathroom updates and whole-floor layout changes, we manage the full remodel — demo, framing, drywall, paint, and finish carpentry. We're hands-on through the whole project and coordinate trades like plumbing and electrical when the scope calls for it.

  • Kitchen remodels
  • Bathroom renovations
  • Basement build-outs
  • Layout and wall changes
  • Doors, trim, and cabinetry
  • Full interior refreshes
04

Exterior Renovations

The outside of your home takes the brunt of Colorado weather. We handle siding replacement, stucco repair, roofing work, and exterior paint to keep your home protected and looking sharp. Durability is the priority — every detail is built to hold up to wind, sun, and snow.

  • Siding repair and replacement
  • Stucco patching and refinishing
  • Roofing repairs and replacements
  • Exterior paint and trim
  • Soffit and fascia work
  • Curb appeal upgrades
